Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how Securedly protects your data and respects your digital rights.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Zero Data Collection Policy

Securedly operates on a strict zero-knowledge principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information processed through our tools.

Information We Don't Collect

Unlike many online services, Securedly is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • We do not collect passwords, usernames, or encrypted data
  • We do not track your browsing activity or usage patterns
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not require account creation or personal information
  • We do not store any data processed through our tools

How Our Tools Work

All Securedly tools operate entirely within your browser:

  • Password Generator: Creates passwords using local entropy sources without transmitting data
  • Privacy Checker: Analyzes your browser settings locally without sending information to servers
  • Username Generator: Generates usernames using client-side algorithms
  • Data Encryption: Encrypts and decrypts data locally using AES-256 without server communication

Technical Implementation

Our privacy-first approach is built into our technical architecture:

  • All cryptographic operations happen client-side in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers during tool usage
  • Our website is static with no backend database
  • We use open-source libraries that respect privacy
  • Code is publicly available for security community review

Third-Party Services

Securedly minimizes third-party dependencies:

  • Fonts: We use Google Fonts which may collect limited usage data
  • Icons: Font Awesome icons are loaded from CDNJS
  • Hosting: Our static site is hosted on privacy-respecting infrastructure

We recommend using browser extensions that block third-party trackers for enhanced privacy.

Open Source Transparency

Securedly is fully open source:

  • All source code is publicly available for inspection
  • Security researchers can audit our implementation
  • Community contributions help improve security and privacy
  • No hidden functionality or backdoors

Security Measures

We implement multiple security layers to protect users:

  • HTTPS encryption for all website communications
  • Content Security Policy headers to prevent XSS attacks
  • Regular security updates and dependency reviews
  • Secure coding practices and vulnerability testing

Legal Compliance

Securedly complies with global privacy regulations:

  • GDPR: Our zero-data collection approach naturally complies with EU data protection standards
  • CCPA: California consumers' privacy rights are respected by our data minimization
  • COPPA: We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13

Your Rights

Under our privacy policy, you maintain full control:

  • Right to access: All processing happens locally on your device
  • Right to erasure: No data is stored, so nothing to delete
  • Right to object: You can stop using our services at any time
  • Right to data portability: You control all data generated

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our tools or services
  • Legal or regulatory requirements
  • Security improvements

Continued use of Securedly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices:

  • Visit our GitHub repository for technical questions
  • Use our contact form for general inquiries
  • Report security vulnerabilities through responsible disclosure
