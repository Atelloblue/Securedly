Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how Securedly protects your data and respects your digital rights.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Zero Data Collection Policy

Securedly operates on a strict zero-knowledge principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data processed through our tools.

Information We Don't Collect

Unlike many online services, Securedly is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • We do not collect passwords, IPs, or encrypted data
  • We do not track your browsing activity or usage patterns
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not require account creation or personal information
  • We do not store any data processed through our tools

How Our Tools Work

All Securedly tools operate entirely within your browser with one exception:

  • Password Generator: Creates passwords using local entropy sources without transmitting data
  • IP Information: Your IP address is sent to third-party services like ipapi.co to retrieve geolocation details. Review their policies for details.
  • Data Encryption: Encrypts data locally using AES-256 without server communication

Technical Implementation

  • All cryptographic operations happen client-side in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers (except IP Information tool)
  • Our website is static with no backend database
  • Code is publicly available for security community review

Third-Party Services

Securedly minimizes third-party dependencies:

  • IP Information: Uses external services like ipapi.co
  • Fonts: Google Fonts may collect limited usage data
  • Hosting: Our static site is hosted on Netlify
