How It Works

AES-256 Encryption

Uses the Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit keys, the same encryption used by governments and security agencies worldwide.

Client-Side Only

All encryption and decryption happens entirely in your browser. Your sensitive data never leaves your device.

Password-Based Keys

Uses PBKDF2 to derive strong encryption keys from your password, making brute-force attacks extremely difficult.

Randomized Security

Each encryption uses random initialization vectors, ensuring identical data produces different encrypted results.
