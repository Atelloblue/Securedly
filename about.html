What We Do

Password Security

Generate cryptographically secure passwords using advanced entropy collection and mathematical chaos algorithms.

Privacy Protection

Analyze and improve your browser's privacy settings with comprehensive fingerprint detection and privacy auditing.

Data Encryption

Protect sensitive information with military-grade AES-256 encryption that processes everything locally on your device.

Our Principles

Zero Knowledge

We never see your data. All processing happens locally in your browser with no server communication.

Open Source

Every line of code is publicly available for review, audit, and improvement by the security community.

Education First

We believe in teaching security concepts, not just providing black-box solutions.

No Compromises

We implement the highest security standards without cutting corners or adding backdoors.
